Shopee Seller Center Redesign

Improve the User Experience for Shop Rating Page



Overview

In this one week design challenge, I redesigned the shopee seller center's shop rating page. By analyzing the current information architecture of this web page, I provided a new design plan in order to improve communication efficiency and store management.

Details

Role: UX Design, Research

By Daokai Lin

Tools: Figma, Invision










Project Information

Shopee is the fastest growing E-Commerce platform in South East Asia, including Singapore, Malaysia, and Taiwan. Shopee provides its sellers with an integrated system to manage orders, optimize the shop, and market the products. My task is to revamp the shop rating page to make sure the sellers can better utilize their resources.

Shop rating is an essential part of Shopee's backstage management system. Many users rely on customer reviews to make a purchase decision, and it's the basis of establishing a trust relationship between shoppers and buyers. Understanding the needs of different types of users is the first step to optimize the shop rating system.

1. Buyers: tend to look at reviews before making a purchase decision; post a product review to tell other consumers the quality of this shop's service and products.

2. Sellers: use this site to know about their customers and what they like; utilize user-generated content to advertise the products and build an excellent reputation.




User Research

After I finished deconstructing the page structure, I conducted several user interviews to understand the seller’s pain points and specific needs. The interviewees include 3 Shopee sellers, 2 Taobao sellers, and 2 Amazon sellers. I made two personas to summarize their user expectations and urgent needs.

用户痛点&设计理念

How to improve customer satisfaction is the main problem. The current design doesn’t help sellers to quickly search for best selling product and find out why buyers gave negative reviews.

The common features of SaaS are helping users to achieve High-Efficiency in Business Communication, manage large amounts of information, and complete tasks in different scenarios. Iteration

The first version of the redesign solely improved the way of how users use the filters. Since the user feedback indicated that they wanted to see the improvement of information architecture and the rating system, I made the second version of the redesign:



Real-time Data: Real-time Data: this section informs the users of new ratings, unread comments, and negative reviews.

Shop Rating: The shop rating section remains on the top right of the screen, but I used bright colors to make it stand out more.

Categories: Added more filter options. Sellers now can sort the reviews by time or product categories.

Filter: I designed a new sidebar filter that shows the average ratings of the products. A red dot indicates the most recent rating a product received, and the quick search function allows sellers to list the most popular items of their shops easily.

Quick Reply: Besides the original reply function, I added a chat button so that the sellers can easily communicate with the buyers.

查看销量/评价: 在按商品查看的页面中，用户可以用销量和评分作为筛选标准，从而更清楚地发现自己店铺内哪类产品有潜在问题，或是找到爆款的品项。最右侧的收藏按钮可以帮助卖家监控最主要的商品，了解买家意见

评价关键词: 显示某款产品的中，差评价情况，并由系统提取关键词，帮助用户分析原因，制定有效的改进计划




总结

我在设计时着重考虑了shopee卖家在使用该系统时最有可能出现的错误和最优先的需求，并在思考如何减少用户学习成本的情况下，对商铺评价界面进行主要功能的改进。

整体看下来，shopee本身存在着大量的新人卖家，他们都需要适当的引导才能正确高效的使用这些功能点。B端产品设计是个漫长且复杂的过程，每一个模块都需要大量的思考，并结合用户场景，才能设计出最好的用户体验的路径
