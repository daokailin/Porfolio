LINKEDLN IOS REDESIGN

DISCOVER VARIOUS PROFESSIONAL MEETING OPPORTUNITIES

Introduction

The objective of this project is to redesign linkedln app's group feature that will increase users' awareness of off-line meetings, professional networking sessions, and resources while ensuring this piece of information is easily accessible and user friendly. Linkedln users should have more opportunities to meet with local professionals and engage in networking.

Identify the Problems

The current Linkedln mobile app is lacking the basic meetup funtion as a social media platform geared to professionals. The existing "My Network" function doesn't provide users with appropriate meeting opportunities to communicate with people who share the same interests and passions in person.

Thus, we are dedicated to implement a new function that helps users to learn knowledgeable skills and industry experience through "real connection."

User Research Methods

1. We sent out online google form surveys to our friends and colleagues who have used linkedln app before. The goal of using this method was to see how college grduates interact with the existing group feature and find out the trends of their expectation or needs of networking experience.

2. Our team conducted two observational interviews collect more in-depth information about these participants' user experience. We asked them to share their experience of meeting with professionals on linkedln, and we recorded how they used the My Network feature to find their interested groups.

From left to right: Timeshifter, Uplift, Sleeptracker.

Many chronic fatigue apps focus mainly on jetlag, but our app looks at chronic fatigue as a whole. We market to a variety of consumers, and have features like the calculator app that benefit students as well as the plan setting that benefits travelers. The products showing above require extensive amount of data input; however, our app only takes 1-2 minutes and is more accurate.

The Design

  • For the design, we were inspired by minimalistic applications. Our main goal is to create an interface that is user-friendly and adovated for any user. However, the color scheme of the application has been changed to a blue color palette. We decided to finalize on softer pastel colors since it correlates with sleep to an extent, but it still enhances light. We are focusing on helping our users with chronic whether it is during the day or during the night, therefore, we wanted our color scheme to correlate with the purpose of the app.

Special Time Management Tools.

After evaluating and considering different options, our team designed the “calculator” feature and plan feature.

  • "Caculating" your study tips
    It can measure the functionality level of a person just by inputting the hour one goes to bed and the hour one wakes up. The calculator will also present potential “action steps” one can take, and how each action can either increase or decrease one’s fatigue level.
  • Build your personal schedule
    Catering to the most fundamental goal of our app to deal with chronic fatigue, we would generate a customized plan tailored to meet specific purposes: cramming, jet-lag, night-shift, etc. Originally, we drew inspiration from the popular activity of cramming among college students.
What the app can do.
  • Performance Tracking games
    These games can reflect the cognitive functions of the player, and thus could enhance our calculator function. They are divided into two categories: one aims to assess the players’ alertness for driving by testing their kinetic visual acuity; the other one aims to assess the player’s alertness for study/work by testing their attention and processing speed via Stroop effect tests. We also included a graph record of the user’s alertness and productivity to increase a sense of continuity.

Final Thoughts and Future Plan

Reflecting on our design process, the most exciting feature we developed was the “calculator”, which we came to an agreement after doing a great deal of user testing and surveys. The calculator function provided our users a smart tool to keep tracking their health condition and productivity.

For our future plan, we want to build more features and have deep integration with more of the fitness trackers on the market to become a universal solution. Additionally, we want to enlarge our user groups and promote this product to working professionals.
